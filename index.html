Explore the scary secrets of Miller Mansion

Miller Mansion is a horror themed, replayable escape room game experince made in Roblox Studio. Enter the haunted halls of the Miller family home where you will find a chilling tale of betrayal and misfortune. Posing as a ghostly AI guide named "Eleanor"(who is the mother of the family), you will make your way through puzzles, associated with procedural generation, to reveal who is the real killer among Anna, Arthur, and Elias which are the sister, father, and son respectively. Amazing for horror fans and any enthusiasts of puzzles, this game's atmoshpere of storytelling and replayability offers new findings and challenges.

Some major features include: Different experiences containing puzzle generation that is procedural; An AI helper guide that contains voice lines, hints that are dynamic, and narration that progresses; five rooms that are connected to one another with interactive objects and lore of the family; many endings based on what is discovered and the amount of time taken to reach the end; and support of multiplayer is included. Built by a team using the Lua programming language, modeling based on 3D, and sound design that is immersive.

Features

What makes Miller Mansion unique

Miller Mansion combines horror storytelling, replayability, and interactive puzzle-solving to create a fresh experience each time you play.

Procedural Puzzle Generation

Key puzzles change between playthroughs, giving players new combinations, clues, and solutions each time they enter the mansion.

Immersive Haunted Environment

Explore interconnected rooms filled with eerie details, environmental storytelling, and interactive objects that reveal the Miller family’s tragic history.

AI Ghost Guide

Eleanor, the spirit of the mansion, acts as a dynamic guide with voice lines, hints, and narration that respond to player progress and discoveries.

Multiple Story Paths

Uncover clues about Anna, Arthur, and Elias to determine who the true killer is, with different discoveries leading to different conclusions and endings.

Timed Escape Gameplay

Your choices and speed matter. The time taken to solve the mystery can influence the outcome and add tension to every run.

Multiplayer Support

Team up with friends to investigate the mansion together, solve puzzles cooperatively, and compare how each playthrough unfolds.
